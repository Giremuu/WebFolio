# Reflection on AI and Its Use

## Claude AI

This portfolio was created with the help of Claude, a conversational artificial intelligence
Its role was to act as both a technical and creative partner — suggesting code, helping structure my ideas, correcting mistakes, and accelerating my development
AI does not replace human thinking, but it can be an incredible amplifier of skills when used wisely

---
## What AI Makes Possible

Significant time savings : it can generate code, suggest fixes, and document functions in just a few seconds
Assisted Learning : understanding a technology becomes faster when you can interact directly with a virtual “mentor”
Enhanced creativity: AI helps explore design, writing, or structural ideas that one might not have imagined alone
Knowledge accessibility : it democratizes technical knowledge for anyone who wants to learn

---
## Important !

Don’t copy stupidly : AI-generated code can contain errors, security vulnerabilities, or licensed content !
Lack of context: AI doesn’t always understand the real constraints of a project or the human needs behind an idea
Risk of dependency : using AI without developing critical thinking means losing the ability to learn and reason independently
Ethical concerns : training data often comes from the web, raising questions about privacy and copyright

---
## To conclude
Artificial intelligence is truly impressive and powerful, but it remains a tool to be used with care and discernment
Use it to learn continuously and to create whatever you imagine !
